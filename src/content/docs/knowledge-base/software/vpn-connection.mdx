## üí° Overview
The VPN (Virtual Private Network) allows you to access the Intranet, shared drives, and internal tools while working remotely.

> **Security:** Never connect to the VPN from a public Wi-Fi network (like a cafe) without ensuring your firewall is active.

##  Installation
If you do not have Cisco AnyConnect installed:
*   <WindowsIcon /> **Windows:** Open **[Company Portal](/docs/reference/software/company-portal/)** and install "Cisco AnyConnect".
*   <AppleIcon /> **Mac:** Open **[Self Service](/docs/reference/software/self-service/)** and install "Cisco AnyConnect".

## üìã Step-by-Step Connection

1.  Open the **Cisco AnyConnect** application on your computer.
2.  In the address bar, type: `vpn.company.com`.
3.  Click **Connect**.
4.  When prompted, enter your:
    *   **Username:** (Your standard login)
    *   **Password:** (Your standard password)
    *   **MFA:** Approve the notification on your Authenticator app.

## ‚úÖ Verification
Once connected, the AnyConnect icon in your system tray will have a small **lock** symbol on it.
